Untitled, 2023. Found photographs, polyester, vinyl, zinc.




CMFMFB, 2023. Aluminum, found photographs, polyester, steel, vinyl, zinc.




Hunting Blind, 2023 Plastic, PVC, wood, zinc.

CMFMFB and Hunting Blind are reproductions of structures found at a Carnegie Mellon University construction site and a recreational hunting ground, respectively, within a 10 mile radius of the Interburden exhibition space. Together in conversation, these two forms blur the discontinuity of Pittsburgh’s working-class identity and its contemporary, tertiary economy. Throughout this work, repurposed vernacular photography from the 1970s-1990s serves as documentation of Pittsburgh's economic transition.
